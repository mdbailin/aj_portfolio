# Welcome

This portfolio is organized around a simple academic structure: a concise home page, a video archive, and a blog for longer-form writing.

## Why this format

- It keeps public-facing work easy to navigate.
- It supports a straightforward writing workflow in TinaCMS.
- It remains compatible with GitHub Pages.

Future posts can be written in Markdown and published through TinaCMS without exposing a custom admin panel on the public site.
